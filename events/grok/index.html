What the fock is Grok?

It’s a three day event sans the how-to sessions from industry leaders. We think a 10 minute session of hard questions from a new face is as good as a lock-tight answer from an old pro. We started Grok because we wanted to learn big, and go big.

Grok is about Greenville

We throw Grok here because we are totally nuts for this place. Beer flows in the streets and the buildings are made from Belgian chocolate – that might as well be true because that’s how we feel about it. Once you see Greenville for yourself, we think you’ll have second thoughts about leaving.
